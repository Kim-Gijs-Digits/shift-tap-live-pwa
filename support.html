About Shift-Tap

Shift-Tap is a work time and shift tracking app designed to help users track work hours, balances and schedules in a simple and efficient way.

The app supports local data storage, backups and PDF exports so users can keep their work information organised.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I export my work hours?

You can export your work hours using the PDF export function available in the app.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. Most core functions of Shift-Tap work offline.

Where is my data stored?

Most data is stored locally on your device unless you export or back it up.

How can I delete my data or account?

You can request data or account removal by contacting the support email.
