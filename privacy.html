Privacy Policy

Shift-Tap — Last updated: March 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Shift-Tap handles information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

Shift-Tap is designed to keep work-related tracking simple and privacy-friendly. The app may store information such as work hours, shifts, balances, exported files, and backup data that you create while using the app.

2. Local Data Storage

Most app data is stored locally on your device so you can continue using core features, including offline use, backups, and exports. This means your information can remain on your own device unless you choose to export or share it.

3. Firebase Services

Shift-Tap may use Firebase services to support app functionality. Depending on the feature being used, Firebase may process technical data required for authentication, app stability, or related cloud functions. Firebase is provided by Google.

4. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to provide the main app functions, such as tracking work time, managing shifts, creating backups, and exporting data.

5. Data Sharing

Shift-Tap does not sell your personal data. Data is not shared with third parties for advertising purposes.

6. Backups and Exported Files

If you use backup or export features, files created by the app may be stored on your device or in locations you choose yourself. You are responsible for how you manage, store, or share exported files.

7. Security

Reasonable steps are taken to keep the app and related services secure. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Choices

You can control your app data by deleting local app data, removing exported files, or uninstalling the app. If account-related deletion or privacy requests are needed, you can contact us by email.

9. Children’s Privacy

Shift-Tap is not intended for children.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updated version will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
